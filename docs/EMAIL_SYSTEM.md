# Email Marketing Engine

## Infrastructure
*   Uses **Resend** as the primary transactional and marketing mail provider.
*   Configured through API keys mapped on the server.

## Automated Flows
*   **Welcome Message**: Sent when a user registers or creates an account.
*   **Lookbook Delivery**: Generates a PDF compilation of saved lookbooks and emails it to the user.
*   **Lead Confirmation**: Emails sales representatives when a customer submits a lookbook inquiry.

## Future Enhancements
*   Newsletter campaign lists.
*   Click and open rate analytics.
